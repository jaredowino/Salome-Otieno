How To Play
Tap two tiles of the same color to match them.

💡 A match only pops when both tiles have an empty neighbor.

🟠 Gold = repositioning needed — clear space first!

🎨 Every level has brand new colors.
⏱ Beat the timer for a Time Bonus.
🔥 Chain matches for a Combo.
❄️ Freeze — stops timer 8 seconds.
🔀 Shuffle — randomises the board.
💡 Hint — highlights a valid pair.
